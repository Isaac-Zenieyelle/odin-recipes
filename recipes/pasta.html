Pasta

Description

Pasta is a traditional Italian dish made from unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, then shaped into various forms like spaghetti, penne, or lasagna.

It is typically boiled and served with a wide range of sauces, such as tomato, cream, or pesto, often accompanied by vegetables, meat, or seafood.

Pasta is a versatile and widely loved comfort food enjoyed globally.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (spaghetti, penne, or any type)
  • Water (for boiling)
  • Salt
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Garlic (optional, for flavor)
  • Tomato sauce or cream sauce
  • Vegetables (e.g., onions, bell peppers, mushrooms)
  • Protein (e.g., chicken, shrimp, or ground beef)
  • Herbs and spices (e.g., basil, oregano, black pepper)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add salt to the boiling water for flavor.
  3. Add the pasta to the boiling water and stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  4. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until it reaches your desired level of doneness.
  5. While the pasta cooks, prepare the sauce by heating olive oil or butter in a pan.
  6. Add garlic, onions, or other aromatics to the pan and saut√© until fragrant.
  7. Mix in your chosen vegetables, protein, or sauce ingredients and cook until done.
  8. Drain the cooked pasta, reserving some pasta water if needed to adjust the sauce consistency.
  9. Toss the pasta with the prepared sauce, adding reserved pasta water if necessary.
  10. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with fresh herbs or grated Parmesan cheese, and enjoy!
