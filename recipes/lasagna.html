lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, savory meat or vegetables, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. Baked until bubbly and golden, it offers a comforting combination of textures and flavors that meld together in each delicious bite. Perfect for family gatherings or special occasions, lasagna is a hearty and satisfying meal that can be customized with various fillings and seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (pasta sheets)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the sauce: In a large pan, cook ground beef or sausage with garlic and onions until browned. Add marinara sauce, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning, and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Make the cheese mixture: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Stir in some grated Parmesan cheese for added flavor.
  5. Assemble the lasagna: In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Place a layer of noodles on top, followed by more sauce, a layer of ricotta mixture, and a sprinkle of mozzarella. Repeat the layers until the dish is full, finishing with a top layer of mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake the lasagna: Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it rest: Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it sit for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
